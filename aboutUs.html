VAT (Value Added Tax) is being introduced across the GCC where Kingdom of Saudi Arabia (KSA) and the United Arab Emirates are the first two nations among the GCC who shall be implementing VAT from January 2018. Other member states of the GCC shall follow implementation of the same shortly.

We at Gulf Tax Agents shall assist businesses in the implementation of VAT from registering businesses to obtain tax registration number (TRN), followed by maintaining accounting books monthly and filing VAT quarterly for registered businesses. We also provide additional services to help businesses deal with the local authorities post VAT filing as and when needed. We are a one stop shop that tailor to all requirements a business may have in relation to the implementation of VAT.

Functions of Gulf Tax Agents

  • 1) Registration to obtain tax registration number (TRN).
  • 2) Assistance with software to electronically maintain sales of businesses.
  • 3) Invoicing.
  • 4) Monthly Book Keeping.
  • 5) Quarterly VAT filing.
  • 6) Storage of VAT returns along with relevant documents for a period of 5 years as required by the government.
  • 7) Assistance with the government bodies during audit and scrutiny.

We aim to provide detailed services which will allow businesses to focus on their core businesses while we take care of all their requirements with relevant government authorities.
