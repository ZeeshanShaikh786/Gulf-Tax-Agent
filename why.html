YOUR TAX AGENT

We at GTA specialize in VAT implementation and focus our work only related to VAT compliances. We have sister companies in other developed economies that are well versed with various taxes and their effects on the economy and various businesses. Our highly qualified and experienced team focusses on individual clients and their needs, to provide them with the best solution. We are VAT specialists where we use all our expertise for one function which is VAT implementation. We shall be your tax agent and shall cover all your requirements from registration to assessments by the Federal Tax Authority (FTA). One stop shop service for all your VAT compliances.

Why hire us?

  • 1) Highly qualified and experienced team that has already worked in other developed economies that use VAT.
  • 2) We understand that you must focus your mind and energy in a competitive market like UAE, therefore we take care of all your VAT related compliances.
  • 3) We shall represent you in front of the FTA if there are any queries by the authority.
  • 4) We keep ourselves updated with the latest news and developments in order to provide you with the best cost effective systems and solutions.
  • 5) We advise and assist in long term tax planning for individual businesses keeping in mind short term and long term goals of companies

GTA’s function

  • 1) VAT consultancy for individual businesses
  • 2) VAT registration to obtain Tax Registration Number (TRN).
  • 3) Assisting you update your systems to make them VAT ready.
  • 4) Periodic bookkeeping.
  • 5) Filing VAT returns quarterly.
  • 6) Assisting you make VAT payments.
  • 7) Assistance/representation during audit by the Federal Tax Authority (FTA).

The Gulf Tax Agent and its quasi-professional associates international chartered accountants firm, headquartered in Dubai.
